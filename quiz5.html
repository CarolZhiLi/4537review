Question 5

‚Ä¢ http://myDomain.com/read.html and http://myDomain.com/fetch.html and http://myDomain.com/get.html
‚Ä¢ only http://myDomain.com/read.html
‚Ä¢ http://myDomain.com/read.html and https://myDomain.com/read.html
‚úÖ Correct Answer

üí° Explanation

Question 10
